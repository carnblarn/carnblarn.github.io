UserRank

For my tech writing final project I created a new algorithm for ranking user content that incoporates a voting system. The crux of the algorithm is that each user's vote has a different weighting based on their previous voting patterns. Users who like content that is later shown to be popular and dislike unpopular will have a higher weight compared to users who vote the opposite.

Since it's not feasible to create a fully-fledged Reddit clone, and more importantly there wouldn't be anyone using it, I have made this page to serve as a proxy for calibrating a user's vote weight. Each piece of content here has been taken from /r/pics with varying final vote tallys that acts as a measure of the "true" quality.
